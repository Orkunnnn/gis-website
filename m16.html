MFıstıkağacı-Beykoz Metro Route

The route is planned to start from M5 Fıstıkağacı station and reach Beykoz centre.

Integration with other routes

Integration with M5 line is planned at Fıstıkağacı station, and integration with metrobus is planned at 15 Temmuz Şehitler Köprüsü station.
